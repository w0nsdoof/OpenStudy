# Cookie Policy for OpenStudy Planner

**Last Updated: December 12, 2025**

## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and enabling certain functionality.

## Our Cookie Policy

OpenStudy Planner uses minimal cookies to ensure essential functionality. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

## Types of Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies
These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off:

1. **Authentication Cookies**
   - Purpose: Keep you logged in to your account
   - Duration: Session-based (when you close your browser)
   - Data: Encrypted user session identifier
   - Necessary: Yes, for logged-in functionality

2. **Security Cookies**
   - Purpose: Protect against CSRF attacks
   - Duration: Session-based
   - Data: Security tokens
   - Necessary: Yes, for security

3. **Preferences Cookies**
   - Purpose: Remember your settings (theme, language)
   - Duration: 30 days
   - Data: User preference settings
   - Necessary: No, but recommended for better experience

## Cookies We Do NOT Use

We do not use any of the following cookie types:
- **Analytics Cookies** (Google Analytics, etc.)
- **Advertising Cookies**
- **Social Media Cookies**
- **Tracking Cookies**
- **Third-Party Cookies**

## Managing Your Cookies

### Browser Settings
You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings:

**Chrome**:
1. Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
2. Choose your preference

**Firefox**:
1. Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
2. Manage your preferences

**Safari**:
1. Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
2. Control your settings

**Edge**:
1. Settings → Cookies and site permissions
2. Manage cookies

### Our Cookie Consent
- We use a simple cookie consent banner
- You can accept or decline non-essential cookies
- Your preference is saved for future visits
- You can change preferences at any time

## Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party services that set cookies on our website. All cookies are first-party only.

## Data Security

All cookies used by OpenStudy Planner are:
- Encrypted where appropriate
- Limited to necessary information only
- Not shared with third parties
- Secure (HTTPOnly and Secure flags where applicable)

## Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable all cookies:
- You will not be able to log in to your account
- Your preferences will not be saved
- Some features may not work properly
- You may need to re-enter information frequently

Essential cookies are required for the service to function properly.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy from time to time. Any changes will be:
- Posted on this page with updated date
- Communicated through our website
- Effective immediately upon posting
