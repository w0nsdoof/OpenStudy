# Accessibility Statement for OpenStudy Planner

**Last Updated: December 12, 2025**
**Effective Date: December 12, 2025**

## Commitment to Accessibility

OpenStudy Planner is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

## Compliance Status

### Current Conformance
- **Standard**: Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA
- **Target**: Partial conformance - some content may not fully conform
- **Last Audit**: December 12, 2025
- **Next Scheduled Audit**: June 12, 2026

### Legal Framework
Our accessibility efforts comply with:
- **Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) of 1990**
- **Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act**
- **EN 301 549** (European Standard)
- **Accessible Canada Act** (ACA)
- **Equality Act 2010** (UK)

## Accessibility Features

### Visual Accessibility
- **High Contrast Support**: Adjustments for users with low vision
- **Text Resizing**: Support for 200% zoom without loss of functionality
- **Color Independence**: Information not conveyed by color alone
- **Focus Indicators**: Clear visible focus states for keyboard navigation
- **Alternative Text**: All meaningful images have descriptive alt text
- **Responsive Design**: Works across various screen sizes and devices

### Hearing Accessibility
- **Visual Notifications**: Important alerts provided visually
- **Caption Support**: All video content includes captions
- **No Audio-Only Instructions**: Critical information not conveyed through sound alone
- **Volume Control**: Users can control or mute audio elements

### Motor/Mobility Accessibility
- **Keyboard Navigation**: Full keyboard accessibility throughout
- **Large Click Targets**: Minimum 44x44 pixels for touch targets
- **No Time Limits**: Sufficient time for users to read and interact
- **Error Prevention**: Confirmation for destructive actions
- **Voice Navigation**: Compatible with voice control software

### Cognitive Accessibility
- **Clear Language**: Simple, understandable content
- **Consistent Navigation**: Predictable layout and interactions
- **Error Messages**: Clear, helpful error descriptions
- **Help and Documentation**: Comprehensive user guides
- **Orientation Aids**: Breadcrumbs and page titles
