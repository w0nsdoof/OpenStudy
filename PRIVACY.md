# Privacy Policy for OpenStudy Planner

**Last Updated: December 12, 2025**
**Effective Date: December 12, 2025**

## Introduction

OpenStudy Planner is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring compliance with international privacy laws and regulations. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our study planning platform.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Email Address**: Only required for account creation and login authentication
- **Username**: Optional display name for your profile
- **Academic Data**: Courses, assignments, and study schedules you create
- **Usage Analytics**: Aggregated, anonymized data for improving our services

### Information We DO NOT Collect
- Personal identification beyond email
- Location data or GPS information
- Financial information or payment details
- Third-party social media profiles
- Contact lists or address books
- Biometric data
- Sensitive personal information (race, religion, political opinions, etc.)

## Legal Compliance Framework

OpenStudy Planner complies with the following laws and regulations:

### International Laws
1. **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)** - EU Regulation 2016/679
2. **California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)** - California Civil Code §1798.100
3. **Brazilian Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD)** - Law No. 13,709/2018
4. **Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA)**

### Accessibility Laws
1. **Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) of 1990**
2. **Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 AA**
3. **Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act**

### Data Protection Principles

#### GDPR Compliance
- **Lawful Basis**: We process data based on legitimate interest (providing educational services) and user consent
- **Data Minimization**: We collect only data necessary for service delivery
- **Purpose Limitation**: Data is used only for stated purposes
- **Accuracy**: We ensure data accuracy and provide correction mechanisms
- **Storage Limitation**: Data is retained only as long as necessary
- **Integrity and Confidentiality**: Robust security measures protect data
- **Accountability**: We maintain comprehensive compliance documentation

#### CCPA Compliance
- **Right to Know**: Users can request what personal information is collected
- **Right to Delete**: Users can request deletion of their data
- **Right to Opt-Out**: Users can opt-out of data selling (though we don't sell data)
- **Non-Discrimination**: We don't discriminate against users exercising privacy rights

## How We Use Your Information

### Core Functionality
- **Account Management**: Provide and maintain your account
- **Service Delivery**: Manage courses, assignments, and study schedules
- **Communication**: Send important service notifications

### Analytics and Improvement
- **Service Improvement**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
- **Performance**: Monitor and optimize application performance
- **Security**: Detect and prevent fraudulent activities

## Data Subject Rights

Under GDPR and similar regulations, you have the right to:

### Access
- Request a copy of your personal data
- Receive information about how your data is processed

### Rectification
- Correct inaccurate personal information
- Complete incomplete personal information

### Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
- Request deletion of your personal data
- Exceptions may apply for legal or legitimate interests

### Portability
- Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- Transfer data to another service provider

### Objection
- Object to processing of your personal data
- Opt-out of direct marketing (if implemented)

### Restriction
- Limit how we process your personal data

## Consent Management

### Explicit Consent
- Clear, granular consent for data collection
- Easy withdrawal of consent at any time
- Consent recorded with timestamps

### Cookie Policy
- Essential cookies only (no tracking cookies)
- Clear cookie consent banner
- Easy cookie preference management

## Data Security Measures

### Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption**: AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- **Transport Security**: TLS 1.3 for data in transit
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access with authentication
- **Regular Audits**: Quarterly security assessments
- **Vulnerability Scanning**: Automated security scanning

### Organizational Measures
- **Privacy Training**: All staff trained on privacy laws
- **Data Protection Officer**: Designated privacy compliance officer
- **Incident Response**: 24-hour breach notification protocol
- **Third-Party Vetting**: Privacy-compliant vendor selection

## International Data Transfers

### GDPR Article 45 Adequacy
- Data transfers only to countries with adequate protection
- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for other transfers
- Privacy Shield equivalent protections

### Data Processing Locations
- Primary servers: EU/US regions
- Backup locations: Encrypted, compliant jurisdictions
- User choice: Region selection for data storage

## Data Retention

### Retention Periods
- **Active Users**: Data retained while account is active
- **Inactive Users**: Data deleted after 2 years of inactivity
- **Legal Requirements**: Extended retention as required by law
- **Analytics Data**: Anonymized after 90 days

### Deletion Process
- Immediate deletion of user-requested data
- Secure wiping procedures
- Third-party notification requirements
- Confirmation of deletion sent to user

## Third-Party Processors

### Approved Vendors
- All vendors sign Data Processing Agreements (DPAs)
- Privacy impact assessments conducted
- Regular vendor audits performed

### Categories
- **Cloud Infrastructure**: AWS/Azure (compliant)
- **Email Services**: Privacy-first providers
- **Analytics**: Anonymized, GDPR-compliant tools

## Children's Privacy

OpenStudy Planner is not directed to children under 16. We do not:
- Knowingly collect information from children under 16
- Market to children
- Use features that appeal specifically to children

If we discover we've collected a child's information, we promptly delete it.
