# Privacy Policy for OpenStudy Planner

**Last Updated: December 12, 2025**

## Introduction

OpenStudy Planner is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our study planning platform.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- **Email Address**: Only required for account creation and login
- **Username**: Optional display name for your profile
- **Academic Information**: Courses, assignments, and study schedules you create

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Analytics**: How you interact with features (for improvement purposes)
- **Device Information**: Browser type and operating system for compatibility

### Information We DO NOT Collect
- Personal identification beyond email
- Location data
- Financial information
- Third-party social media profiles
- Contact lists

## How We Use Your Information

- **Core Functionality**: To manage your study schedule and assignments
- **Account Management**: To provide and maintain your account
- **Improvement**: To analyze usage patterns and enhance features
- **Security**: To protect against unauthorized access

## Data Protection & Security

- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS)
- **At Rest Encryption**: User data is encrypted when stored
- **Access Controls**: Strict internal access restrictions
- **Regular Backups**: Encrypted backup systems
- **Anonymization**: Analytics data is anonymized

## Data Sharing

We **DO NOT**:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share personal information with advertisers
- Use your data for marketing purposes
- Share data with educational institutions without consent

## Your Rights

- **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Correction**: Update your personal information
- **Deletion**: Delete your account and all associated data
- **Export**: Download your data in a portable format
- **Opt-out**: Disable analytics tracking

## Data Retention

- Account data is retained until you delete your account
- Deleted accounts are permanently removed within 30 days
- Anonymous analytics data may be retained indefinitely

## International Users

Your data may be stored on servers outside your country. We ensure:
- Compliance with GDPR and other privacy regulations
- Adequate data protection standards
- Clear consent for international data transfers

## Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of significant changes:
- Email notification for major policy updates
- 30-day notice period before implementation
- Posted updates on our website
